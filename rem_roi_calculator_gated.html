How this estimate is calculated +
Recovered Labor Value = Number of Field Electricians × Fully Loaded Electrician Cost × Time Recovered From Material Handling. Recovered Staff Value = Number of Office Staff × Fully Loaded Office Staff Cost × Time Recovered From Purchasing Workflow. Material Savings = Annual Material Spend × (Material Waste Reduction % + Material Cost Savings %). Total Annual Gross Value = (Recovered Labor Value + Recovered Staff Value + Material Savings) × Adoption Rate. Net Annual Value = Total Annual Gross Value − Annual Platform Cost. Breakeven Months = (Annual Platform Cost ÷ Total Annual Gross Value) × 12 + 4 Month Implementation.
